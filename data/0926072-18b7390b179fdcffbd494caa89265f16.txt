Hey everyone!!!! So, as promised, here is our massive pack of our favorite tracks from 2011(in no particular order). 2011 was a great year for music and an even greater year for OHD. We grew from being nothing but an idea between friends to a world-wide blog run by multiple members that share similar tastes, style and most importantly, love for music and each other. I’m very proud, and very happy to be apart of such a wonderful community. Sorry for the mushiness, hadda get it out of the wayyyy. Here is our beloved list of the BEST OF 2011!!!!!

Thanks to Tom, Eddie, and Alejandro for getting this in on time. I appreciate it :). ENJOY & HAPPY NEW YEAR!!!!
