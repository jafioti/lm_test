Ready for a new favorite snack? With the Super Bowl right around the corner, I thought you all would love this great recipe for easy Keto Cheesy Garlic Bread. It is amazing! It’s very filling and also very addicting! I made this easy keto cheesy garlic bread up as I went along, and it turned out incredible. This is sure to be a crowd pleaser for your next low carb get together. Add 1/4 cup of Tomato sauce to dip in, and you have a real treat!

It’s also unbelievably fast and easy! Enjoy delicious, cheesy, garlicy goodness in under half an hour.

br>

Easy Keto Cheesy Garlic Bread Ingredients:

br>

3/4 Cup – Mozzarella Cheese Shredded

1/2 Cup – Almond Flour

2 tbsp – Cream Cheese

1 – Large Egg

Garlic Powder to taste

Basil to taste

Oregano to taste

Himalayan Pink Salt to taste

Black pepper to taste

2 tbsp – Kerrygold Butter

2 – Cloves Garlic

Parchment Paper

Rolling Pin

Baking sheet

Toppings (For example Cheese, Bacon, Jalapeno, ect.)

br>



br>

Directions – Easy Keto Cheesy Garlic Bread

br>

1. Place Mozzarella Cheese, seasonings, and Cream Cheese in microwave safe mixing bowl.

br>

2. Microwave for 60 seconds.

br>

3. Stir with a spatula.

br>

4. Mix in almond flour and egg.

br>

5. Stir with spatula until it forms a ball of “dough”.

br>



br>

6. Tear 2 large pieces of parchment paper and place “dough” ball in between them.

br>



br>

7. Using a rolling pin, roll out dough into desired shape and thickness. I suggest thicker, rather than skinnier.

br>

8. Grease baking sheet and preheat oven to 425 degrees.

br>

9. Transfer rolled out dough to baking sheet.

br>

10. Using a fork, poke holes throughout crust to avoid bubbling.

br>

11. Bake crust for 8 minutes at 425 degrees.

br>

12. Remove crust from oven and use a fork to poke holes in any bubbles in crust.

br>

13. Place crust back in oven for 2 minutes.

br>

14. Place Kerrygold Butter and minced garlic in a microwave safe mug. Microwave garlic and butter for 40 seconds.

br>

15. Remove crust from oven and paint with warm garlic butter. Liberally apply garlic butter to entire crust.

br>

16. Top crust with mozzarella cheese and any desired toppings. Sprinkle with basil and parmesan if desired.

br>



br>

17. Place back in over for 3-5 minutes at 425 degrees or until cheese is golden brown.

br>

18. Remove from oven, cut and enjoy!

br>



br>

I’d love to see how yours turns out. These could be really good with real bacon bits on them, sausage, pepperoni, or any topping you like! You could also dip in Ranch, or use 1/4 cup of tomato sauce for dipping, if desired. This has quickly turned into one of my favorites!
